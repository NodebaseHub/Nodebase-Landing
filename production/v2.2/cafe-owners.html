Your next regular is already looking for you.

Nodebase connects independent cafés with a growing community of engaged café hoppers in Hong Kong — people who discover places through trusted word-of-mouth, not algorithms.

How it works

Someone in our community visits your café. They share it back to the group. Other members see a real recommendation from a real person — and come find you.

Community member recommends your café → Café hoppers visit → Visit gets shared back → More hoppers discover you

This is community-driven discovery. No paid placement. No commission taken. No algorithm deciding whether you appear today or not.

Running an independent café in Hong Kong today takes more than great coffee.

Delivery platform commissions keep rising. Margins keep shrinking. And the customers who genuinely care about what you do — who come for the craft, not the discount — are hard to reach and harder to hold on to.

Delivery apps give you transactions. They don't give you relationships.

Where Nodebase fits

Nodebase is a community-first café discovery platform built for independent cafés in Hong Kong. We are not a delivery app. We are not a review site. We are not another platform taking a cut of your revenue.

We are building a community of people who actively seek out independent cafés — and share what they find with each other. People who visit because someone they trust recommended it. People who come back.

What that means for your café:

Genuine discovery from people already looking for places like yours

Word-of-mouth that travels further than your immediate neighbourhood

A direct relationship with customers who care about independent cafés

Visibility that isn't dependent on ad spend or platform ranking

Nodebase works alongside what you already do. It builds the layer that delivery platforms never could: a real community that genuinely wants independent cafés to thrive.
