For Café Owners

Running an independent café today isn't just about great coffee.

It's about navigating rising delivery platform commissions, shrinking margins, and limited control over how customers find and return to your café.

Delivery apps can bring visibility — but they don't help you build lasting relationships with the people who genuinely care about what you create.

Where Nodebase fits

Nodebase is a community-first platform designed to help independent cafés strengthen direct relationships with coffee lovers who value craft, transparency, and local businesses.

We focus on what delivery platforms don't:

– Turning occasional visitors into repeat regulars
– Helping customers discover cafés beyond algorithms and discounts
– Giving café owners more ownership over their customer relationships

Nodebase isn't here to replace delivery platforms overnight.
It's here to help you rely on them less, over time.

Built with café owners, not just for them

We're building Nodebase alongside independent café owners — starting with a small early community shaping how the platform works.

If you believe independent cafés deserve more control, visibility, and sustainable growth, we'd love to build this together.
